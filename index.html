Course Overview

The Diploma in Physiotherapy is a comprehensive program designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the field of physical therapy. Through practical training and academic learning, students will become proficient in rehabilitating patients, improving mobility, and promoting overall physical wellness.

Duration: 2 years

Eligibility: High school diploma or equivalent

Curriculum

  • Introduction to Physiotherapy
  • Anatomy and Physiology
  • Biomechanics
  • Exercise Therapy
  • Electrotherapy
  • Orthopedics and Traumatology
  • Neurological Rehabilitation
  • Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation
  • Sports Physiotherapy
  • Clinical Practicum

Career Prospects

Graduates of the Diploma in Physiotherapy program can pursue various career paths, such as:

  • Physiotherapist in hospitals and clinics
  • Rehabilitation specialist
  • Sports therapist
  • Healthcare consultant
  • Private practice owner
