Course Overview

The Diploma in Physiotherapy is a comprehensive program designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the field of physical therapy. Through practical training and academic learning, students will become proficient in rehabilitating patients, improving mobility, and promoting overall physical wellness.

Duration: 2 years

Fee: USD 600 Per semester

Eligibility: High school diploma or equivalent

Curriculum

    • Introduction to Physiotherapy: This module provides an overview of the field of physiotherapy, its history, and its role in healthcare. Students learn about the principles of rehabilitation, patient assessment, and treatment planning. The course also covers professional ethics and communication skills essential for effective patient care. This module is taught using a blended learning approach, with access to online lectures from eminent professors, interactive AI bots for practice, and offline hands-on sessions.
    • Anatomy and Physiology: A detailed study of the human body's structure and functions. Topics include the skeletal, muscular, nervous, cardiovascular, and respiratory systems, with an emphasis on their relevance to physical therapy. The course uses high-quality virtual dissections, AI tools for anatomy quizzes, and offline lab practicals guided by top experts in India.
    • Biomechanics: This subject delves into the mechanics of body movements and the application of physics principles to human motion. Students learn about joint mechanics, posture analysis, and gait patterns. Students benefit from simulation software, AI-powered assessments, and offline workshops on advanced biomechanics techniques.
    • Exercise Therapy: Focused on therapeutic exercises to restore and enhance physical function. Students learn to design individualized exercise programs for patients with various conditions, such as musculoskeletal injuries and chronic diseases. The learning is enriched with virtual exercise demonstrations, AI feedback on technique, and in-person training by seasoned physiotherapists.
    • Electrotherapy: This course introduces the use of electrical modalities for pain management, muscle stimulation, and tissue healing. It covers the operation and safety of equipment like TENS units, ultrasound machines, and interferential therapy devices. Includes AI-guided simulations for device handling and in-person labs to ensure mastery of practical applications.
    • Orthopedics and Traumatology: Covers the diagnosis, management, and rehabilitation of orthopedic conditions and injuries. Students learn about fractures, dislocations, post-surgical care, and injury prevention techniques. Features online case studies, interactive AI consultations, and offline clinical sessions with top Indian specialists.
    • Neurological Rehabilitation: Focuses on the assessment and treatment of patients with neurological disorders such as stroke, spinal cord injuries, and multiple sclerosis. Techniques like neuroplasticity training and motor control therapy are emphasized. Blended learning includes AI-based neurological assessment tools, online lectures, and offline practicals.
    • Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation: A specialized module that trains students in rehabilitating patients with heart and lung conditions. Topics include exercise testing, breathing techniques, and lifestyle modifications. Includes virtual simulations for emergency scenarios and offline clinical practice in supervised settings.
    • Sports Physiotherapy: Concentrates on the prevention, assessment, and rehabilitation of sports-related injuries. Students learn about athletic taping, performance enhancement, and return-to-sport protocols. Blended modules include online workshops from international experts, AI-guided performance analytics, and on-field training sessions.
    • Clinical Practicum: A hands-on training experience where students work under the supervision of licensed physiotherapists in real-world settings. They apply theoretical knowledge to treat patients in hospitals, clinics, and community health centers. This module is enhanced by AI-powered diagnostic tools, online progress tracking, and offline patient interaction sessions.

    Blended Learning Approach: This program employs a unique blend of online and offline training methods. Students have access to highly eminent professors from India, AI-powered bots for learning assistance, virtual tools for practice, and in-person sessions for skill refinement. This ensures flexibility, high engagement, and hands-on expertise for all learners.
